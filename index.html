About us

The Toronto Women's Data Group brings together a network of women interested in data science, analytics, data engineering, mathematics and statistics, as well as members from the broader community who support our goals.

We provide an open, welcoming, and fun environment for women to network, learn, and innovate together. We're open to women in or aspiring to be in data-driven fields, and we provide a platform for women to share their knowledge and learn from one another.

We also recognize the value of allyship, and welcome men to join us in support of building up our community.
